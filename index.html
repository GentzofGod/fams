Welcome to the Fingerprint-Based Attendance System

Here is how the system works:

The following are the roles available and their duties:

Super Admin

  • Registers and manages HODs.
  • Configures system settings.
  • Monitors system usage and security.

Head of Department (HOD)

  • Adds and assigns courses.
  • Monitors student attendance.
  • Generates attendance reports.

Lecturer

  • Records student attendance.
  • Generates attendance reports.
  • Ensures attendance compliance.
